How does SpineWise work?

Get Comfortable

Start by settling into the smart chair comfortably, ensuring your back is supported and your feet are flat on the floor.

Connect your Chair

Use your mobile or PC to connect the smart chair to the SpineWise web app.

Stay Mindful

As you sit, pay attention to any feedback from the chair via the web app to adjust your sitting position accordingly

Features

Explore the innovative functionalities designed to enhance your posture, comfort, and overall spinal health with the Spinewise Smart Chair.

Ergonomic Design

Contoured seat and backrest provide optimal support to maintain proper spinal alignment promoting good posture.

Real-time Feedback

Integrated sensors and AI algorithms detect posture deviations and provide instant feedback, encouraging users to correct their posture.

Detailed Analysis

The chair's AI compiles detailed analytics of the user's previous sitting habits, including duration, frequency, and posture variations.

Predictive Analytics

By analyzing historical sitting data, SpineWise predicts potential posture-related issues and provides recommendations to prevent discomfort.

User Profile Customization

Based on the user's input regarding spinal health and body dimensions, a user profile is created adjusting optimal settings.

Adjustable Features

To accommodate various body types and preferences, SpineWise is equipped with a range of adjustable features.
